This is a dataset of practice questions asked by U.S. Immigration officers at the interview stage in the process of getting a Green Card through Marriage. Interviews are arranged by U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services after couples file a "Petition for Alien Relative" I-130 form. The questions are sourced from several immigration lawyers' public websites.

This dataset is a preliminary work, with two viable futures:
