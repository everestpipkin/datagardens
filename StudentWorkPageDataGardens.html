Fantasy Networks :

Assignment: Create a “fantasy” communication network in line with our conversations around future networks, alternative networks, and minimal and low-tech computing. You may elect to build from past network histories or start from scratch designing a communication network that rises from an alternate tradition than the internet as we know it.

The project may take any form, including (but not limited to): written speculation, a game, a video, a powerpoint/slide presentation, a demo, a performance, technology/code that works, and technology/code that does not work.
